IT Support

This is one of the roles that can be found in non-technical companies. IT Support provides technical support to internal staff. This support ranges from issues with software applications and hardware used within the company.

Coding

You do not need to code much if not at all.

Working in teams

If you find yourself working in large companies you are likely going to work in a team.

Working alone

You will likely be working alone in smaller companies or if you decide to freelance.

Impact

IT support helps staff members to solve technical issues that might otherwise render them unproductive.

Managing a team

If you find yourself in a large establishment for a while or with increasing experience you might end up leading an IT support team.

Dealing with customers

Your primary focus will be to help your staff with their technical issues.

Learning Curve

You will have to be well versed with popular software and hardware used in most companies.
