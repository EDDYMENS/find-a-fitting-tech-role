Indie Developer

An Indie developer is a software developer who works on a software product all by themselves without the intention to build a team.

Coding

You will have to code to build your product. The specific of what kind of coding you will be doing depends on the product you will be working on.

Working in teams

This is non-existent.

Working alone

This is what it's all about

Impact

The impact of what you work on will depend on the product idea.

Managing a team

This is non-existent

Dealing with customers

Since it's just you running the show you will have to deal directly with the end-users of your product. If you make an income through say google ads you might not have to deal with end customers.

Learning Curve

How much you have to learn depends on what you are building. As an indie developer, you can spread out your learning curve which can be a good thing.
